Weekly Meetings

Each week the 7th hosts a weekly meeting for all of the sections. During these meetings each section will to working towards their badges. Each badge is different and requires different skills so all of the scouts will learn a range of different and new skills. Our Group prides ourselves on making sure that all scouts get involved in all Scout Meetings. The link below shows the badges the Scouts work for at meetings each week.

Scouting Activities

Outside of weekly meetings out Scouts do plenty of different activities. Each year there is at least 2 district camps spanning over the weekend where all the scout groups in the district camp together. The 7th also do an annual week camp in August. This camp involves doing different each day such as guild scrambling, wood cutting and more. There is multiple district competitions each year such as quizzes and sports.
