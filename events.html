7th Gateshead's Upcoming Events

2020

District Camp - 28/08-31/08

District Camp is a camp where all Scouts and Explorers can get invloved. During this weekend each Scout Group will set up a range of activites and challenges for all the Scouts to do. These activites involve lighting fires, wood cutting and backwoods cutting which all contribute to badges. These camps give the Scout groups a good opportunity to meet other scouts and make new friends. It is overall a varied experience which is enjoyed by all.

Annual Cub/Beaver Quiz - 10/09

Each year the Cubs and Beaver sections do a district quiz involving every group in the district. The Quiz involves 25 teams of 6. There is 5 rounds where groups will be knocked out until there is 2 teams left. These teams will go head to head for the title. Snacks and drinks will be provided for all pf the kids at a half time break.

7th Week Camp - 19/09-26/09

The 7th Group do annual week camp in August with all of the sections.This camp is great opportunity to experiencea traditional side to being a camp. The sections will be doing activites specific to their section. For example the Scouts will be learning to Wood cut. Each day the sections will do different sorts of activities to make sure the scouts are doing something new and exciting.

Camping Competition - 02/10-04/10

The District Camp Competition pits the Cubs and Beavers against other groups. Each group will plan their menus activities and work before. The programmes will be given to the judges and they will rate each of the groups on how well they will follow the programme. This competition is a great way to get the Beavers and Cubs in planning and organsing at an early age.

Confido - 13/11-15/11

Confido is a huge weekend of activities with a great atmosphere. 1200 Scouts, Explorers and Guides will camp together for two nights, eat in our centrally catered, 60m marquee and enjoy two evenings of entertainment including bands, a disco, campfires and our own cinema. There really is something for everyone at Confido.

Christmas Party - 18/12

All sections and parents are welcome to the 7th Gateshead Scouts Christmas Party. The Christmas Party will involve parties games, quizzies and more. There will be food and drink provided for all the kids and parents. A raffle will also be going on in the background to help raise money for new equipment for the group.

2021

Winter Camp - 15/01-17/01

Winter Camp is a camp just for the Explorers. The Explorers will given the opportunity to go on a range of activities similar to at the Confido camp. There will be a disco tent available for all of the Explorers. All Explorers will be provided with food and drink all weekend. Winter Camp is a great opportunity for the Explorersto do an camp by themselves and being able to bigger and harder activities that wouldn't be suitable for other sections.

Badge Day - 16/01

Badge Day gives the Cubs and Beavers an opportunity to be able to get all sorts of badges in one day. Last year the kids were able to get up to 7 badges. Some of the badges to gain this year will be the cooking badge and environmental badge. This day is a great day for the kids to learn new skills and meet kids from other groups.

Presentation Night - 04/02

Presentation night is a annual event for the 7th Gateshead Scouts. This Presentation night will be where all of the kids at the night will be presented with all of the badges they have achieved within the year. All parents are welcome to see the presentation. At the presentation there will be scout of the year for each section chosen. This award represents the scout who has got the most out of being a scout this year.
